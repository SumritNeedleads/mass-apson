Mechanism CBP

Mechanism CBS

  1. The unique bucket shape collects oil and scum floating on the liquid surface of the coolant.
  2. The collected coolant, oil, and scum are discharged from the chute of the main body to the separation tank.
  3. Coolant is separated from scum/oil in the separation tank using the difference in specific gravity between the coolant and scum/oil. Then, the oil is discharged to the oil box, and the coolant is returned to the tank again.
