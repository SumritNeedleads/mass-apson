Portable Wet-to-dry Filtration (LAX)

Filters & dries coolant impurities all in one compact unit

Features

  1. Capable of filtering both sediment and floating contaminants with special gas-liquid two phase flow pump.
  2. Compact and portable design to easiliy filter various machines and locations.
  3. Continuous removal and replenishment of coolant / liquids:
    No large filtration tank required or risk of overall and damage.
    Non-interruptive filtration reducing costly downtime.
  4. Debris and fine particulates dried for low cost disposal and increased coolant life.
  5. Bag filter for convenient handling and disposal.
