Special Drump-pod for Fine Sludge wih VDF (DPV)

Compact size, unified with special drum-pod and VDF

Features

  1. 1. Sludge is concentrated in the drum pod

      – Inline installation in small spaces is possible (above, beside tank).

      – 2 liter and 6 liter sizes are available

  5. 5. High efficiency filtration
      – Removes 90% of 10 μm aluminium sludge (specific gravity 2.7).
      – Reduces industrial waste.
      – Reduces inital and running costs.
