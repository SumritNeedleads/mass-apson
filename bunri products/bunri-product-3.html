Microfiltration filter conveyor BAL

Precision drum filter conveyor with high-rigidity sintered material filter.
Optimum for cutting chip processing of non-magnetic materials.

Features

  • The filtration accuracy is as high as 20 Î¼m 90% or more, so secondary processing is not required.
  • This product does not require any secondary processing, and supports high-pressure pumps of up to 3 MPa.
  • The filter uses a high-rigidity sintered material filter that is difficult to tear.
  • It is automatically cleaned by shower cleaning to prevent clogging.
  • The chip discharge port of the conveyor has a structure that drains liquid, which ensures that chips are drained before being discharged.
    This processing reduces the load to carry out coolant (means that coolant is discharged together with chips).
  • This product does not use any consumables such as cartridge filters or paper filters, so no industrial waste is generated.

Mechanism

  1. The dirty liquid flows onto the conveyor.
  2. The dirty liquid is filtered when it passes through the rotating sintered material filter.The clean liquid is sent from the inside of the sintered material filter to the outside of the main body.
  3. Chips adhering to the surface of the sintered material filter are shower-cleaned from the outside of the filter.Clogging is prevented by showering the filter that has come out of the liquid and performing automatic cleaning.
  4. Chips that have settled on the bottom of the conveyor are conveyed by the scraper.
  5. Chips are temporarily stored in the conveyor outlet, drained, and then discharged.
